Akira Seino is a Project Researcher at [Smart Robot Design Lab.](https://srd.mech.tohoku.ac.jp/) in [Tohoku University](https://www.tohoku.ac.jp/en/), Japan. He was a Project Assistant Professor at [Mechatronics Lab.](http://www.rb.sss.fukushima-u.ac.jp/) in [Fukushima University](https://www.sss.fukushima-u.ac.jp/en/), Japan (2019-2021). He was a Research Officer at [TransGP](https://www.transgp.hk/), Hong Kong (2021-2025). He was also a Visiting Research Associate at [The University of Hong Kong](https://www.hku.hk/) (2022-2025). 

He obtained his Bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering at [Yamagata University](https://www.yamagata-u.ac.jp/en/), in Yamagata, Japan. He obtained his Master's and Ph.D. at Tohoku University, in Japan.

His main research topic is in the area of mechanical desgin for robots, power-assisted system, and manipulation.

He enjoys fishing, listening music, and [Photography](https://www.instagram.com/a.jono744/).
